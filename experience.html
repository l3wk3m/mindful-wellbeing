Welcome to Behind

As we each go through our days, we accumulate different kinds of baggage

Maybe an offhand comment from a stranger in a coffee shop

Maybe your boss didn't appreciate the effort you put into a project like you'd hoped they would

It's a fact of life that each one of us will face challenges from day to day

These land on us all in different ways and when we don't manage these stresses optimally

We end up accumulating baggage along the way

Here at Behind we try and teach you

How to leave that baggage...
